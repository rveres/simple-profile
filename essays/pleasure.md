The following is an essay written as I applied to university in Fall 2019.

///

*We know you lead a busy life, full of activities, many of which are required of you. Tell us about something you do simply for the pleasure of it. (100 words or fewer.)*

I don’t think many people call their front porch at midnight “paradise.” I believe they’re mistaken. Almost every night, I sit outside and gaze up at the night sky teeming with orbs of light. The simplicity of darkness comforts me: chirping insects provide a symphony for the light show unfolding above.

The stellar panorama strikes me equally humbling and awe-inspiring. There’s something beautiful about knowing I’m just a tiny part of the universe while still the consequence of billions of years of random events that have aligned just so to allow me to sit here living, breathing, wondering.
